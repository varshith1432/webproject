Games and Reqirments

Half-Life: Alyx
OS: Windows* 10
CPU: Intel® Core™ i5-7500 processor or better
RAM: 12GB of system memory
Graphics card: NVIDIA* GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD* Radeon RX 580
GPU RAM: 6GB of video memory

Built from the ground up for VR, Half-Life: Alyx makes creative use of lighting, perspective, and environmental effects to construct thought-provoking puzzles and cultivate tense encounters with enemies. Using Alyx’s gravity gloves to grasp objects from a distance is a revelatory experience enhanced by the sophistication of modern processors.

Resident Evil 3
OS: Windows* 10 (64-bit)
CPU: Intel® Core™ i7-3770 Processor or better
RAM: 8GB of system memory
Graphics card: NVIDIA* GeForce GTX 1060 or AMD* Radeon RX 480
GPU RAM: 45GB of available space

The first thing you’ll likely notice about the Resident Evil 3 remake is how detailed Raccoon City is, even in its state of ruin and disrepair. Swaths of neon light descend on heaps of wreckage while shadowed alleyways conceal pockets of horror. Giving in to the allure of Raccoon City’s beauty is the best way to set yourself up for the scares that lie ahead.

Call Of Duty:war zone
OS: Windows* 10(64 bit)
CPU: Intel® Core™ i7-9700 processor or better
RAM: 16GB of system memory
Graphics card: NVIDIA* GeForce GTX 2080 Super
GPU RAM: 175 gb of Available space

Call of Duty: Warzone, the free-to-play battle royale addition to last year’s Call of Duty: Modern Warfare, is well-equipped to foster once-in-a-lifetime feats of FPS acumen. With up to 150 players on a map at once, the possibilities for combat encounters are endless, but can also be hardware-intensive. Between the high-quality textures and effects and the importance of draw distances, having the recommended components can help ensure the best possible play experience.

Ori and the will of the wisps
OS: Windows* 10
CPU: Intel® Core™ i5-6600k or better
RAM: 8GB of system memory
Graphics card: NVIDIA* GeForce GTX 970 or AMD* Radeon RX 570
GPU RAM: 20GB of video memory

The designers behind Ori and the Will of the Wisps built on the signature, hand-painted look of its predecessor, Ori and the Blind Forest, by adding dizzying amounts of depth to every scene. Levels expand with layers of visual detail, while a new dynamic lighting system fills tranquil groves and desert caverns with color.

Gear Tatics
OS: Windows* 10
CPU: Intel® Core™ i5-8600k processor or better
RAM: 8GB of system memory
Graphics card: NVIDIA* GeForce GTX 970 or AMD* Radeon RX 580
GPU RAM: 45GB of video memory

Gears Tactics, the first-ever turn-based tactics entry in the Gears franchise, offers a robust suite of in-game settings that allow the player comprehensive control of even the most granular of the game’s features. The game’s minimum frame rate setting dynamically adjusts the game’s resolution to maintain a stable rate of choice, while other settings manage sophisticated shadows and reflections.
